INTERACTION DESIGN?

You might be be asking yourself, "What is an interaction designer and what do they do?"

Well, I'm a creative problem-solver who focuses on the needs and desires of the user to create a meaningful experience. An experience free of facepalms, head itching and smashing of the device.



HOW DO I ACCOMPLISH THIS?!

1. PROBLEM

Stating the problem or goal is essential. Framing the problem statement can be difficult because you don't want to limit your exploration.

2. RESEARCH

Discovering benchmark studies is critical to the feasibility of our ideas. Knowing what other competitiors are doing isn't bad either.

3. IDEATION

This stage usually consists of frustration, anger and tears until we finally reach a breakthrough that gets our brain in on the right track.



4. PROTOTYPE

You won't know if it works unless you try! Testing out your idea early can save a lot time, resources and headaches for everyone. If it doesn't work, start over!

5. REVISIONS

Whether it works on the first or hundredth attempt. There's always room for improvement.

6. SOLUTION

Solving and completing the project can feel rewarding. Besides feeling satisfied and proud of yourself you finally get to sleep! Hooray! We did it!
